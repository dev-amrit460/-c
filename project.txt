&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Wildlife - A Definition
</h1><hr>
<p align="left">Wildlife traditionally refers to non-domesticated animal species.but has<br>
come to include all plants,fungi and other organisms that grow or live<br>
wild in an area without being introduced by humans.It can be found in all<br>
ecosystems.Deserts,forests,rain forests,plains,grasslands and other<br>
areas including the most developed urban sites,all have distint forms of<br>
wildlife.</p>
